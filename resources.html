Resources

Explore our curated collection of research, videos, frameworks, and external links on Corporate Social Responsibility in higher education.

Research Papers & Publications

  • UN PRME — Sharing Information on Progress Report 2023 PDF

    Annual report documenting how 800+ business schools worldwide are integrating responsible management principles into curricula.

  • AASHE STARS — Sustainability Tracking, Assessment & Rating System PDF

    A comprehensive framework used by 1,000+ higher education institutions to measure and report sustainability performance.

  • ISO 26000 — Guidance on Social Responsibility Guide

    The international standard providing guidance on how organizations, including universities, can operate in a socially responsible way.

  • GRI Standards — Global Reporting Initiative Guide

    The world's most widely used sustainability reporting standards — a critical tool for universities publishing CSR reports.

Videos & Webinars

  • What is CSR? — Corporate Social Responsibility Explained Video

    A concise explainer on what Corporate Social Responsibility means, its pillars, and why it matters for institutions and businesses.

  • Sustainability in Higher Education — TED Talk Video

    A TED Talk exploring how universities can lead the transition to a sustainable future through research, policy, and campus action.

  • The Role of Universities in Achieving the UN SDGs Webinar

    An expert-led webinar on how higher education institutions are aligning their missions with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.

External Websites & Articles

  • UN PRME — Principles for Responsible Management Education Website

    Official website of the United Nations initiative inspiring responsible management education across the globe.

  • AASHE — Association for the Advancement of Sustainability in Higher Education Website

    The leading organization in North America for campus sustainability, offering resources, benchmarking tools, and a global community.

  • United Nations — Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) Website

    The 17 global goals adopted by world leaders to end poverty, protect the planet, and ensure prosperity — a core framework for CSR in education.

  • Journal of Cleaner Production — CSR in Education Research Article

    Peer-reviewed academic journal publishing cutting-edge research on sustainability, CSR practices, and their integration in educational institutions.

Frameworks & Standards

  • SDG 4 — Quality Education Framework

    The UN's framework for inclusive, equitable quality education — a foundation for CSR-driven academic policy.

  • SDG 17 — Partnerships for the Goals Framework

    Encourages universities, governments, and businesses to build multi-stakeholder partnerships that advance CSR and sustainability agendas.

  • UN Global Compact — Ten Principles Framework

    A voluntary initiative for companies and institutions to commit to responsible business practices in human rights, labor, environment, and anti-corruption.
